About Us

State of Art Infrastructure

The college is located in the heart of South Delhi. It is in a sprawling lush green area of 10-plus acres. Approximately 20 meters is the distance from Sukhdev Vihar Metro Station to Don Bosco Institute of Technology. For Delhi Metro, it is the Magenta Line and for the road, it is the MP Road. Air Conditioned classrooms await the students with smart boards, whiteboards, and air purifiers. Wi-Fi-enabled campus adds beauty for the students to make their learning better. A smart library with an e-library system and discussion room adds variety for the students to choose the library as one of the prime locations for them to sit and make their study progress. The computer center is equipped with the latest technology and trained resource personnel is always ready to assist students. One full-sized Foot Ball Ground along with a Cricket Ground, two Basket Ball Grounds with two Volley Ball Grounds and a Badminton Court add beauty to their sense of purpose. Parking for all in the basement makes life secured and safe. All these and more are awaiting you.
