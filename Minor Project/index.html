Don Bosco Institute Of Technology

Don Bosco Institute of Technology (DBIT) ( A Unit of Don Bosco Group of Educators), Okhla is adjacent on the bank of Sukhdev Vihar Metro Station, Okhla, South Delhi, 110 025. This college offers you opportunities to grow professionally and personally. It is affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Dwarka, New Delhi.

About Us

Course We Offer

Explore our programs designed to nurture your passion for technology and equip you with the skills to shape the future.

Bachelor of Computer Applications(BCA)

The BCA program prepares students for a dynamic and rewarding career in the rapidly evolving IT industry by equipping them with the essential knowledge and practical skills to thrive in both software and hardware environments.

Bachelor of Business Administration(BBA)

The BBA program equips graduates with the tools and insights necessary to pursue successful careers in business, start their own ventures, or continue with higher education in business and management.

Bachelor of Commerce(B.COM)

The Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of commerce, finance, and business management. Through a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical applications, this course equips students with the skills needed to excel in the world of business, accounting, economics, and entrepreneurship.
